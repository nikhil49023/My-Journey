🎓 Concept Deep Dive

In PyTorch, a Tensor is more than just an array. It is a mathematical entity that carries its own history (via Autograd) and can be teleported between your CPU and GPU with a single command.

The Golden Rule of Shapes

Deep learning is 90% debugging shape mismatches. Always check tensor.shape before performing operations like matrix multiplication.
